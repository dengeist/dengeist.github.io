Introduction

I'm a former creative type who is trying a more sensible, capitalism-approved career in front- and backend web development. Maybe I'll try design too; who knows.

My interest in programming came as a natural extension of my love for human languages. I love understanding the working parts of a big system and figuring out how to manipulate those working parts to make a system work the way I want it to.

Where I'm From

My hometown is in a sleepy, conservative armpit of the Mojave Desert, blessedly far away from Los Angeles but within access to it if we want to visit it for its good parts. We have more Joshua trees per capita than we have reasonable adults, but hey, the views are pretty neat.

More About Me

What are your favorite hobbies?

I like to game in my spare time--RPGs are a favorite of mine. I'm friends with quite a few talented actors, so I spend a lot of time watching theatre. When I'm not gaming or admiring the arts, I'm teaching myself to bake fancy things.

I would love to get paid to test water slides (it's a real thing!) but I do not live in a perfect world, so being able to work as a full-stack web developer would be nice too. Bonus dream points: the same job, but in Germany.
